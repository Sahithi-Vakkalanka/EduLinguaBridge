About EduLingua Bridge

Our Mission

EduLinguaBridge aims to bridge the gap between educational resources and students by providing innovative features to enhance learning experiences. Our platform offers real-time subtitles, text accessibility, effective doubt resolution, and lecture recording to make learning more accessible and effective for everyone.

Features

  • Real-Time Subtitles: Get live translations and subtitles during lectures to improve comprehension.
  • Text Accessibility: Access course materials in multiple formats to accommodate various learning needs.
  • Effective Doubt Resolution: Get your questions answered promptly with our interactive doubt resolution system.
  • Lecture Recording and Review: Revisit recorded lectures to review content and reinforce learning.
